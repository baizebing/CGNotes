### Midpoint Method

- Compute Euler step (a)
- Compute derivative at midpoint of Euler step (b)
- Update position using midpoint derivative (c)

$$
x_{mid} = x(t) + \Delta t/2 \cdot v(x(t),t)
$$

$$
x(t+\Delta t)=x(t)+\Delta t \cdot v(x_{mid},t)
$$

算出中点，用中点的速度来替代原来的速度
